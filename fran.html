Meet Fran

Founder & Heart
of Fran's Flowers

Francine "Fran" Miller founded Fran's Flowers in 1995 with a vision to create a boutique flower shop that not only offered beautiful arrangements but also gave back to the community. With a deep love for flowers and a passion for helping others, Fran has dedicated her life to brightening the lives of San Francisco residents, one bouquet at a time.

"Flowers have a unique way of bringing joy and connection. I'm grateful to share that joy with our community every day." ♥ - Fran Miller
